Why Karnobat matters

One of the largest Jewish necropolises in Bulgaria, used by Karnobat and nearby communities.

Historic tombstones are endangered by neglect, erosion, vandalism, and theft.

We focus on dignified preservation, documentation, and long-term protection.

Mission, Vision & Guiding Principles

Who we are and what we stand for.

Mission

SHOMER is dedicated to the preservation, protection, and restoration of the historic Jewish cemetery of Karnobat, Bulgaria, ensuring dignity for those buried and safeguarding cultural and historical legacy for future generations.

Vision

To transform the Karnobat Jewish Cemetery from an endangered site into a protected, documented, and respectfully maintained place of memory, education, and historical continuity.

Guiding Principles

  • Dignity of the deceased is paramount.
  • Non-disturbance of graves; no invasive intervention without proper authority.
  • Historical accuracy and respect.
  • International collaboration.
  • Transparency in operations and governance.

Tagline Options

  • Preserving memory. Restoring dignity.
  • Guardians of forgotten heritage.
  • Where history is remembered, not abandoned.

Objectives & Organizational Structure

How we work: restoration + research + outreach + protection.

Core Objectives

  • Physical Preservation: stabilize and restore displaced or damaged tombstones; reduce erosion, vandalism, and theft; protective measures.
  • Documentation & Research: catalogue and digitize tombstones; preserve Hebrew and Ladino inscriptions; academic partnerships.
  • Cultural & Educational Outreach: public education; exhibitions; diaspora engagement (Canada, Israel, Europe).
  • Advocacy & Legal Protection: collaborate with authorities on heritage protection; long-term stewardship.

Organizational Structure

  • Board of Directors: President, Vice-President, Director of Finance, Director of Research & Archives, Director of Restoration & Field Operations.
  • Advisory Council (optional but powerful): historians, legal experts, conservation specialists, community leaders.
  • Operational Teams: Field Ops (Bulgaria), Research & Digital Archive, Communications & Outreach, Partnerships.

Founding Narrative

The Karnobat Jewish Cemetery, established centuries ago and once containing over a thousand tombstones, is among the most historically significant yet severely neglected Jewish heritage sites in Bulgaria. SHOMER was founded by students across Canada, Bulgaria, and Israel to respond with urgency, respect, and long-term stewardship.
